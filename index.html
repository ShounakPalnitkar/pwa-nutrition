Avocado Toast with Eggs
Ingredients (Serves 2)
  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
Instructions
  1. Toast the bread until golden and crisp.
  2. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  3. Add lemon juice, salt, and pepper to the avocado and mash with a fork.
  4. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  5. Crack the eggs into the pan and cook to your preference (about 3 minutes for sunny side up).
  6. Spread the mashed avocado on the toast and top with the cooked eggs.
  7. Sprinkle with red pepper flakes if desired and serve immediately.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories: 320 kcal | Protein: 15g | Carbs: 30g | Fats: 18g

Fiber: 10g | Vitamin C: 20% DV | Iron: 15% DV

Quinoa Buddha Bowl
Ingredients (Serves 2)
  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, carrots)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 2 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the chickpeas and mixed vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Spread on a baking sheet and roast for 20-25 minutes until tender.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup, and 2 tbsp water to make the dressing.
  5. Divide the cooked quinoa between two bowls.
  6. Top with roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and avocado slices.
  7. Drizzle with tahini dressing and serve.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories: 450 kcal | Protein: 18g | Carbs: 55g | Fats: 20g

Fiber: 15g | Iron: 25% DV | Vitamin C: 90% DV

Spicy Lentil Soup
Ingredients (Serves 4)
  • 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook for 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Add cumin, paprika, and chili flakes. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add lentils, diced tomatoes, and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
  6. Stir in spinach and cook for 2 minutes until wilted.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with crusty bread if desired.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories: 280 kcal | Protein: 20g | Carbs: 40g | Fats: 5g

Fiber: 18g | Iron: 35% DV | Vitamin A: 120% DV

Grilled Salmon Salad
Ingredients (Serves 2)
  • 2 salmon fillets (about 6 oz each)
  • 4 cups mixed greens
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. Preheat grill or grill pan to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush salmon fillets with 1 tbsp olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill salmon for 4-5 minutes per side until cooked through.
  4. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, avocado, cherry tomatoes, and red onion.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together remaining olive oil, lemon juice, honey, and Dijon mustard to make the dressing.
  6. Drizzle dressing over the salad and toss gently.
  7. Divide salad between two plates and top with grilled salmon.
  8. Serve immediately.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories: 380 kcal | Protein: 30g | Carbs: 15g | Fats: 25g

Omega-3: 2.5g | Vitamin D: 80% DV | Vitamin C: 60% DV

Greek Chicken Wrap
Ingredients (Serves 2)
  • 2 whole wheat tortillas
  • 1 chicken breast, cooked and sliced
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cucumber, grated
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions
  1. In a small bowl, mix Greek yogurt, grated cucumber, garlic, lemon juice, salt, and pepper to make tzatziki sauce.
  2. Heat tortillas according to package instructions.
  3. Spread 2 tbsp of tzatziki sauce on each tortilla.
  4. Divide chicken slices between the two tortillas.
  5. Top with tomatoes, red onion, lettuce, and feta cheese.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt and pepper if desired.
  7. Roll up the tortillas tightly, tucking in the sides as you go.
  8. Cut in half and serve immediately.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories: 400 kcal | Protein: 35g | Carbs: 30g | Fats: 18g

Calcium: 25% DV | Iron: 20% DV | Vitamin B12: 45% DV

Berry Smoothie Bowl
Ingredients (Serves 1)
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1/4 cup granola
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Fresh berries for topping
Instructions
  1. In a blender, combine frozen banana, mixed berries, and almond milk.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy, adding more almond milk if needed.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Top with granola, chia seeds, almond butter, and fresh berries.
  5. Drizzle with honey if desired.
  6. Serve immediately with a spoon.
Nutrition Facts (per serving)

Calories: 250 kcal | Protein: 8g | Carbs: 45g | Fats: 10g

Fiber: 12g | Vitamin C: 120% DV | Antioxidants: High
